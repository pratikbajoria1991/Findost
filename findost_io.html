Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about investing with Findost.

What is Findost? +

Findost is an AI-powered investing platform that helps every Indian grow their wealth through mutual funds, SIPs, and fixed deposits — simply and securely. It features PaisaGuru, an AI financial advisor available 24/7 in Hindi and English.

What is the minimum SIP amount on Findost? +

You can start a SIP on Findost with as little as ₹500 per month. There are no hidden charges and zero distributor commissions — you only invest in direct plans that maximise your returns.

How do I start investing on Findost? +

Starting is simple: (1) Sign up free with your mobile number, (2) Complete digital KYC using PAN & Aadhaar in under 2 minutes, (3) Pick your financial goal, (4) Start your SIP. The entire process takes less than 5 minutes with no branch visits or paperwork.

Is Findost SEBI registered and safe? +

Yes. Findost Financial Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is registered with SEBI as an Investment Adviser under SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013. Your data is protected with 256-bit SSL encryption and two-factor authentication. FDs are DICGC insured up to ₹5 lakhs.

What is PaisaGuru? +

PaisaGuru is Findost's AI-powered financial advisor chatbot. It provides personalised fund recommendations, SIP planning, tax-saving advice under Section 80C, and portfolio reviews — available 24/7 in plain Hindi and English. No jargon, no hidden agenda.

What returns can I expect from mutual funds on Findost? +

Equity mutual funds on Findost have historically delivered 12–18% average annual returns over 10+ years. Fixed deposits offer up to 9.1% p.a. from partner banks. Past performance is not indicative of future results; all investments are subject to market risks.
